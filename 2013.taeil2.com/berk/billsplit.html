Bill Split is a group tip calculator. If one person orders a steak and another orders a salad, they shouldn't be expected to pay the same amount. This web app solves this problem.

While this app is pretty simple, it showcases many of the skills I learned at UC Berkeley. I worked on the interaction design, graphic design, and coding. I believe it's very important to be well rounded, since communication across disciplines is much better when you understand what they go through.
